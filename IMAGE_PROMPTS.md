# Image Generation Prompts for The Barn Website

Use these prompts with GPT (DALL-E, Midjourney, or similar) to generate images for The Barn website. Save all images in the `images/` folder.

## Image 1: Hero Section - Workshop Space (Primary)
**File name:** `hero-workspace.jpg` ⭐ **REQUIRED - This is the main hero image**

**Prompt:**
```
A warm, inviting modern workshop space with exposed brick walls, large windows showing greenery outside, and natural light. In the foreground, two people are seated in comfortable modern armchairs facing away from camera. In the background, a presenter with a beard stands in front of a large whiteboard, gesturing and pointing at content written on it. The room has wooden tables, potted plants, and a cozy, collaborative atmosphere. Warm earthy tones, browns, and beiges. Professional photography style, slightly blurred background effect, warm lighting. Modern coworking/workshop aesthetic. High quality, realistic.
```

## Image Specifications

**Recommended Settings:**
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 4:3 for hero images
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD) or higher
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Style:** Professional photography, realistic, warm lighting
- **Color Palette:** Warm earthy tones (browns, beiges, warm whites)

## Color Scheme Reference
- Primary browns: #8B4513, #A0522D
- Warm beiges: #F5E6D3, #FFF8F0
- Natural greens: From plants and windows
- Warm lighting: Natural daylight with warm tones

## Key Elements to Include
- ✅ Exposed brick walls
- ✅ Large windows with natural light
- ✅ Modern, comfortable furniture (armchairs, wooden tables)
- ✅ Whiteboard or presentation area
- ✅ Potted plants
- ✅ Warm, inviting atmosphere
- ✅ Diverse people (when showing people)
- ✅ Professional but cozy aesthetic

## Notes
- All images should maintain the warm, earthy, inviting aesthetic
- Avoid cold, sterile, or corporate-looking spaces
- Focus on the "barn" feeling - warm, welcoming, community-oriented
- Images should feel authentic and real, not overly staged
- Natural lighting is preferred over harsh artificial light
